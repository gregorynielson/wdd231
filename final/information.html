About Golfing to Give

At our core, we believe golf can be more than just a game—it can be a force for good. Our club brings together players of all skill levels to compete in tournaments hosted at a variety of courses, all while supporting meaningful causes. Each event is designed to create a fun, competitive atmosphere where every swing contributes to something bigger, with proceeds benefiting local and national charities. By partnering with generous sponsors and passionate players, we're building a community that shares a love for golf and a commitment to making a positive impact both on and off the course.

Memberships

The membership is designed to make participating simple and rewarding. With a single annual membership, players receive entry into all scheduled tournaments throughout the season, eliminating the need for separate event registrations. Members also enjoy priority access to tee times, ensuring a smooth and convenient experience on tournament days. By joining, you're not only gaining exclusive access to great golf events—you're also supporting the charitable mission behind every round played.

Our Courses

Our featured courses are carefully selected to deliver an exceptional experience for every event. Each location offers a unique blend of scenic beauty, thoughtful design, and well-maintained conditions that appeal to golfers of all skill levels. From wide, inviting fairways to strategically placed hazards, these courses strike the perfect balance between fun and challenge—rewarding great shots while keeping every round engaging from the first tee to the final putt.

Charities We Help

The charities we support represent a wide range of meaningful causes, from helping families in need and supporting youth programs to advancing healthcare and protecting the environment. Each organization is chosen for the real, measurable impact it makes in people's lives and communities. Through every tournament, players contribute to efforts that provide essential resources, create opportunities, and improve lives—turning each round of golf into a chance to make a lasting difference.
